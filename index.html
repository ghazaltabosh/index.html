Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand your own and others' emotions and deal with them consciously and calmly. This skill improves how you handle situations and increases your success in your relationships, work, and daily decisions.

Why is emotional intelligence important?

  • Helps you control your emotions.
  • Makes your connection with others deeper.
  • Improves your daily decisions.
  • Reduces stress and increases self-confidence.
